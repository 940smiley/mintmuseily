## 2026-02-28 - Enhanced Minting Feedback & Accessibility
**Learning:** For blockchain-based applications, immediate feedback (like loading spinners for transaction requests) and post-confirmation UI (like success modals) are critical for reducing user anxiety during long-wait operations. Focus management in modals is essential for keyboard accessibility.
**Action:** Always include 'useWaitForTransactionReceipt' to trigger success feedback and manage focus when displaying modals.
